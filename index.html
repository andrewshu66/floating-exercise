Merit Hero

We're a local organization aiming to identify, locate, and lessen the education gap between students.

Our Mission

This idea was inspired by the growing education gap between the United States and the rest of the world and between high and low-privileged American students.

Our board of partners realized that, while we will not be able to change the skewed national education system, we will be able to help close the education gap by inspiring students to work harder in school.

How it works

1. Sign up.    2. Invite donors.    3. Get rewards.

Merit Hero is simple - family and friends want you to improve in school, and you want to earn something for all of your hard work. Family and friends can sponsor you with a reward for achieving an academic goal, giving you extra motivation to work harder in school and them the satisfaction of helping their favorite student.
